Technological Differentiation of Rule-based Chatbots vs. Langly

Rule-based Chatbots

Rule-based Chatbots operate on a clearly defined set of rules based on "if-then" logic. Each user input is matched against this rule set, and responses are based on preprogrammed scripts. Rule-based systems are limited in their flexibility and can only handle predictable, well-defined tasks.

AI Chatbots

AI Chatbots utilize machine learning and natural language processing (NLP) to understand and respond to user queries. Unlike rule-based bots, AI Chatbots can learn from company data and improve their responses over time. They can handle unexpected user queries and can be deployed in more complex interaction environments.
